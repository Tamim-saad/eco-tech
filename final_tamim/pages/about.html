🚨 The Triple-Blind Crisis

Bangladesh's rivers face three simultaneous crises that are invisible to traditional monitoring:

🏭

60% Industrial Pollution — নদী দূষণ

2,500+ factories discharge into rivers. Among them, identifying the exact polluter is impossible without spectral evidence.

🏗️

40% Riverbanks Seized — নদী দখল

Illegal land filling shrinks rivers by 3-5 meters/year. Slow and incremental — invisible without decade-long satellite comparison.

🌊

10K Hectares Lost/Year — নদী ভাঙন

River erosion displaces 1M+ people annually. Monsoon clouds block optical satellites when erosion is worst.

💡 Our Solution

NodiWatch deploys AI-powered satellite surveillance (Sentinel-2, Sentinel-1 SAR, Landsat) through Google Earth Engine to create the first unified monitoring platform for all three river threats simultaneously.

🔬

Pollution Fingerprinting

Spectral index classification (NDTI, Red/Blue) + Bayesian factory attribution

📐

Encroachment Detection

CNN water segmentation comparing 10-year satellite boundaries

🌊

Erosion Forecasting

SAR coherence change detection works through monsoon clouds

🧠 AI Architecture

Layer 1: Satellite Processing

  • • Sentinel-2 (10m, 5-day revisit)
  • • Sentinel-1 SAR (VV+VH, cloud-free)
  • • Landsat 9 OLI (thermal bands)
  • • Automated cloud masking & atmospheric correction

Layer 2: AI Classification

  • • Random Forest for pollution type classification
  • • CNN U-Net for water pixel segmentation
  • • SAR coherence for erosion detection
  • • Bayesian model for factory attribution

Layer 3: Evidence & Alerts

  • • Court-ready evidence package generation
  • • Real-time alert system with SMS integration
  • • Dashboard visualization & interactive maps
  • • Citizen ground-truth validation
